Who are we ?

As bioinformatics students at the Faculty of Computers and Information Technology, we are passionate about machine learning and its potential applications in healthcare. Our goal is to assist ophthalmologists in diagnosing eye diseases more accurately through the development of a website. In the future, we hope to expand our platform to support other medical specializations as well. We strive for accuracy and precision in all aspects of our work.
